About Justin

More Than an Advisor.
A Trusted Partner.

Justin Williams is the Founder & CEO of Justinvest, built around one simple mission: helping clients make smarter decisions through life's biggest purchases. Whether purchasing a home, protecting assets with insurance, or finding the right vehicle, clients receive honest guidance, personalized service, and long-term support.

Justin is a licensed REALTOR® with Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices Fox & Roach on the Scott Farnan Team, serving Delaware, Pennsylvania, and Maryland. He is also a licensed Insurance Producer with the Nichols Agency – Allstate and an Automotive Sales Consultant with Preston CDJR of Wilmington.

Outside of business, Justin is a proud father of twin boys and a youth football coach in Middletown, Delaware—experiences that shaped Justinvest's core values: honesty, accountability, and lasting relationships.
